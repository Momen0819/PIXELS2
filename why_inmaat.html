Inmaat AlBilad Co.

was created in 2005 as a specialized Food and Beverage company.

It aims specifically at enriching the region’s immerging markets with innovative restaurant concepts, fast food and exceptional casual dining franchises.

We have chosen Riyadh to start, and by 2005 , Indian Palace open its doors to Customers.

- In 2007, Indian Style was Opened to serve Indian food for the upscale Category of customers.

- In 2009, Samakatee, a Seafood Concept was founded to serve mouthwatering international Dishes.

- In 2010, Jouriyat, a Private and Corporate catering company.

- In 2012, Indian Island saw the light as a Contemporary Indian Cuisine.

- In 2015, Indian Palace second Branch with a Capacity of more that 1000 pax.
